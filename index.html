// 01 — About

What is QuadraAI?

An AI-powered Financial Intelligence Platform developed under QuadraForge Finance — combining artificial intelligence, financial analysis, market data, and educational guidance into one unified assistant.

Instead of saying

"Buy this stock."

QuadraAI explains:
"Here is how this company works, here are the risks, this is how to evaluate it, and here are the factors you should consider before investing."

Core Philosophy

Education first.
Recommendations never.

QuadraAI focuses on building financial literacy and informed decision-making rather than promoting speculation or blind recommendations.

Vision

Democratize financial intelligence by making AI-powered financial education available to every student, investor, and professional across India.

Mission — Help users
Learn finance
Analyze markets
Understand investments
Plan finances & build wealth
Make informed decisions through responsible AI

Instead of one large AI prompt, QuadraAI uses specialized agents — different experts answering different financial questions for higher quality responses.

🧠
General AI Agent

Handles everyday financial conversations, budgeting, money management, saving strategies, financial literacy, and career advice.

Budgeting Saving Financial Literacy Career Advice Money Management

Research Agent

Deep dives into company research, industry analysis, financial concepts, market explanations, investment education, and economic analysis.

Company Research Industry Analysis Investment Ed. Economic Analysis

Portfolio Agent

Reviews portfolios, assists with asset allocation, goal planning, risk analysis, investment diversification, and financial planning.

Portfolio Review Asset Allocation Risk Analysis Goal Planning
